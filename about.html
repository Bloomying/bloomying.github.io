Introduction

Welcome to my art exhibition "Bloomying", a play on my name, Ying. I love the coincidence of the pronunciation of my name with "ing", so I coined "Bloomying" as the name of this exhibition to convey "Ying is blooming" doing art.

I come from a math and statistics background, worked as an image algorithm engineer and deep learning practitioner, and now I explore the beautiful intersection of art and technology - where mathematical precision meets artistic intuition, where code dances with creativity.

The Journey & Philosophy

Two years ago, when I decided to become an artist, I couldn't imagine what I would create — all I had was the faith and determination for my artist dream.

Now, I have a whole website filled with diverse works across various mediums, from delicate heat shrink crafts to code-generated patterns, from traditional Chinese brush paintings to iPad digital illustrations, from AI fashion designs to interactive experiences, from captured moments in photography to experimental video art.

You can explore them by themes (including "Blooming with Pride", "My Vigor. My Heartbeat.", and "My Fantasy. My Dreamland.") or by mediums (including digital paintings, photography, code-based art, crafts, traditional Chinese brush painting, AI art, video art, music, and writing).

I am amazed by how creativity unfolds when we dare to follow our inner calling. This journey validates my philosophy: when we believe in the existence of a beautiful world, it comes into being. Through this belief, I've truly transformed my dream into reality.

Through countless hours creating visual arts, crafts, music, and numerous experiments across different mediums, I have found passion and vitality in life. Whether solving mathematical problems, developing innovative algorithms for technical challenges, or making art - to me, they're all like engaging puzzle games. Perhaps life's most important puzzle is ourselves, and our most essential task is to understand and continually refine who we are. In my life, technology and art are not opposing forces; rather, they are complementary languages that decode the greatest mystery of life: ourselves.

Perhaps the most profound artwork we can create is our own life - having the courage to recognize and nurture our gifts, to follow our inner calling, and to find joy in authentic self-expression. Through my creative practice, I hope to inspire others to embark on their own journey of self-discovery and creation.

Featured Works

Fountain

"Fountain" is an interactive generative art piece where you can create filled circles of random colors by hovering the mouse. The size of each circle depends on the duration of the mouse hover.

With a single click, these simple circles transform into complex and beautiful fluid flows, creating an enchanting fountain-like effect that demonstrates how simple elements can evolve into intricate patterns through computational art.

Pink Flowers

A serendipitous capture on my way home, a surprise gift from randomness. Walking in a light drizzle after watching a movie, singing along to "Tian Hei Hei" with an empty soda bottle as percussion, I captured this slightly blurry yet beautifully imperfect moment of pink flowers by the path. Despite multiple attempts later, I could never recreate this shot - perhaps a testament to the unique beauty of spontaneous moments.

Read the full story about this magical evening and the story behind this photograph.

Illuminated

"Illuminated" is my first iPad painting and marks the beginning of my artistic journey. I remember that night - feeling a bit down, I picked up my iPad and started making random strokes. Gradually, I found myself completely immersed in the creative process, as the world around me seemed to fade into peaceful silence. Hours passed unnoticed until nearly 2 AM, when exhaustion finally pulled me away from the screen. This simple act of creation, lasting just a few hours, filled me with an energy that radiated through the following days.

The piece captures an interplay of light and shadow, as if illuminating darkness itself - a reflection of my own state that night, finding light within shadows. The title "Illuminated" carries dual meaning: both the visual effect of light breaking through darkness in the painting, and the moment my life itself was illuminated by rediscovering art.

This piece represents more than just a painting; it's the spark that ignited my artistic path. From this moment on, I felt my life being lit up with new purpose and possibility.

Ripples of the Sun (太阳的涟漪)

An AI-generated fluid animation that captures the ethereal dance of light and motion. Like ripples on a cosmic pond, the warm golden hues flow and merge in an endless cycle of transformation, reminiscent of the sun's reflection on gentle waters.

This piece serves as the landing page video, welcoming visitors with its mesmerizing fluidity. It symbolizes how creativity, like sunlight, creates ripples that spread outward, touching and transforming everything in their path. Just as the sun's warmth nurtures life, art has the power to illuminate, inspire, and bring warmth to the human experience.

Freedom

An original earring design expressing personal liberation

Like a bird soaring towards its mountain, this earring design embodies the spirit of personal liberation. Inspired by the metaphor of flight and the journey towards self-discovery, it represents that moment when we spread our wings and dare to pursue our authentic path. Just as birds naturally seek their heights, this piece celebrates our innate desire to rise above limitations and fly towards our own mountains - our deepest aspirations and truest selves.

A mesmerizing collection of patterns generated through recursive algorithms, where code becomes a brush to paint intricate designs. Each pattern emerges from mathematical precision yet carries an air of mystique, as if revealing the hidden geometry of dreams.
